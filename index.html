The workflow

One select. One click. Done.

The old way wastes seconds every time. Ask AI collapses the entire loop to a single action.

01

Select any text

Highlight a word, sentence, or whole paragraph on any webpage. The button appears instantly near your selection.

02

Click Ask AI

A floating button appears right next to your selection. One click chooses your preferred AI and fires off the prompt.

03

Get the answer

Your selected text is auto-typed and sent to ChatGPT, Gemini, or Perplexity. The AI responds immediately.

04

Switch anytime

Open the options page to swap between ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. Your preference is saved per browser.

Features

Built for real research

Every feature exists to shrink the distance between a question and an answer.

Floating button

A non-intrusive Ask AI button appears next to selected text on any webpage, disappears when not needed.

Right-click menu

Works via the browser context menu too — right-click any selection and choose your AI provider.

Multi-AI routing

Route to ChatGPT, Gemini, or Perplexity. Provider-specific injection handles each platform's unique editor.

Auto send

Your selected text is injected into the AI editor and submitted automatically — you never touch the input.

Manifest V3

Built on Chrome's latest extension architecture for better security, performance, and long-term compatibility.

No data collection

Open source. Your text stays between you and the AI provider you choose. Nothing is logged or stored remotely.

Get started

Install in 60 seconds

No Chrome Web Store required. Load it directly as an unpacked extension.

  1. 1
    Download the repo — click the button below, then unzip the folder anywhere on your computer.
  2. 2
    Open Chrome or Edge and navigate to chrome://extensions
  3. 3
    Toggle on Developer mode in the top right corner of the extensions page.
  4. 4
    Click Load unpacked and select the project folder you downloaded.
  5. 5
    Click the Ask AI icon in your browser toolbar to open the options page and choose your preferred AI.

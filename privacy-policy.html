Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

```

Overview

Ask AI Browser Assistant allows users to send selected webpage text to AI services such as ChatGPT, Gemini, or Perplexity.

Information We Collect

  • User-selected text that the user chooses to send to an AI service.
  • Extension settings stored locally, such as preferred AI provider.

How Information Is Used

Selected text is used solely to fulfill the user's request and is transmitted to the AI provider chosen by the user.

Data Storage

Settings are stored locally using browser storage. The extension does not sell user data.

Third-Party Services

When users submit text, that content may be processed by third-party AI providers including OpenAI, Google, or Perplexity according to their respective privacy policies.

Data Sharing

Data is shared only with the AI service explicitly selected by the user. No data is sold or used for advertising purposes.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the extension developer through the support information provided in the extension listing.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the extension after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
```

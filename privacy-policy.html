Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Overview

Ask AI Browser Assistant enables users to send selected text from webpages to AI services such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. The extension only processes information necessary to perform this functionality.

Information We Collect

The extension may process:

  • Text explicitly selected by the user and submitted through the extension.
  • Extension preferences and settings stored locally on the user's device.

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit browsing history, passwords, payment information, cookies, account credentials, or personal files.

How Information Is Used

Selected text is used solely to fulfill the user's request to interact with an AI service. The extension does not use submitted content for advertising, profiling, analytics, or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Extension settings are stored locally using the browser's storage APIs.

The extension does not maintain external databases and does not store user-submitted content on developer-controlled servers.

Third-Party Services

When a user chooses to send selected text to an AI service, that text may be transmitted directly to the selected provider, including:

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT)
  • Google (Gemini)
  • Perplexity

The processing of submitted content is governed by the privacy policies and terms of the respective provider.

Data Sharing

Information is shared only with the AI service explicitly selected by the user for the purpose of generating a response.

The extension does not sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

Security

The extension requests only the permissions necessary to provide its functionality and follows browser security practices for handling user-selected content.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the extension after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the support information provided in the extension listing.
